What are Deepfakes?

Deepfakes

A deepfake is an image, or a video or audio recording, that has been edited using an algorithm to replace the person in the original with someone else (especially a public figure) in a way that makes it look authentic.


Recent trends

Deepfake technology is often utilized for entertainment purposes, such as creating humorous content for platforms like TikTok, where users may engage in lighthearted and amusing video posts. However, with the introduction of this fairly new AI technology, people with nefarious intentions came along to take advantage of it.
