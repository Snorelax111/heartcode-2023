Who to reach out to if you are a victim of DeepFake?

Singapore Police Force (SPF)

The mission of the SPF is to prevent, deter and detect crime to ensure the safety and security of Singapore. The SPF has noted the insurgence of impersonation crimes like Deepfake in the recent years. If you have information related to such cases, please submit it online via the button below.

Report Deepfake
TOUCH Cyber Wellness

TOUCH Cyber Wellness (TCW) is a leading non-profit agency that advocates cyber wellness. We provide counselling for children, youth, adults, couples & families on various issues including cyber wellness, excessive device use, addiction, delinquency, marital, parenting, emotional & interpersonal issues.

Reach out
Belle, Beyond the Label helpbot

If you or someone you know is experiencing cyberbullying, Belle, the Beyond the Label helpbot, can provide assistance. Belle is an interactive platform that offers mental health resources and services in a private and convenient manner.

Tips against Deepfake

Tips and Tricks to prevent yourself from being a victim:
  • Use a VPN to mask your IP address
  • Be cautious about the amount of personal information you share online. Minimize the details available publicly, especially on social media platforms.
  • Avoid accepting friend or connection requests from unknown individuals. Masqueraders may use fake profiles to gain access to personal information
